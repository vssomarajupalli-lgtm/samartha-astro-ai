# Samartha Astro AI — Master Questionnaire & Mathematical Prediction Matrix

## 1. Core Architectural Weightage Formula (40:30:30)
Every single-focus query evaluates a final structural probability score out of a maximum 100 points based on three precise classical layers:
1. D1 Bhava Lord (House Owner) Status [40% Weight]: Evaluates the Bhava Lord's baseline strength, placement, and dignity in the D1 Chart.
2. Nisarga Karaka (Subject Significator) Status [30% Weight]: Evaluates the inherent functional karaka planet for that specific life event.
3. Shodasha Varga & Divisional Strengths [30% Weight]: Broken down mathematically into:
   - D9 Navamsha Strength: Allocated exactly 15% weight.
   - Specific Divisional Chart (Varga): Allocated exactly 10% weight mapped directly to the query's domain (e.g., D10 for Career, D4 for Property).
   - Vimshopaka Overall Shodashavarga Pool: Allocated exactly 5% weight based on collective placement dignity across all 16 charts.

## 2. Global Modifiers & Guardrails
- Karaka Bhava Nashaya Filter: If the natural Karaka occupies the target query house directly (e.g., Jupiter in 5th house for progeny, Venus in 7th house for marriage), apply an automatic structural modifier/delay flag.
- Pushkara Navamsha & Vargottama Check: Planets occupying identical signs in D1 and D9 (Vargottama) or falling within Pushkara Navamsha degrees receive automatic positive modifiers within their respective 30% varga allocation pool.
- Progeny Gender Rules: Evaluate odd signs (Male) vs even signs (Female) based on exact planet/pada configurations in the D7 chart.
