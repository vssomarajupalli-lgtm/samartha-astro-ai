# Samartha Astro AI — Master Questionnaire & Mathematical Prediction Matrix

## 1. The 40:30:30 Weightage Architecture
Every single-focus query evaluates a final structural probability score out of a maximum 100 points based on three precise classical layers[cite: 1]:

1. **D1 Bhava Lord (House Owner) Status [40% Weight]:** The primary structural anchor[cite: 1]. Evaluates the Bhava Lord's baseline strength, planetary placement, and placement dignity in the D1 Chart[cite: 1].
2. **Nisarga Karaka (Subject Significator) Status [30% Weight]:** Evaluates the inherent functional karaka planet for that specific life event[cite: 1]. If the Karaka is exceptionally strong, it can mitigate a moderately weak Bhava Lord[cite: 1].
3. **Shodasha Varga & Divisional Strengths [30% Weight]:** Broken down mathematically into[cite: 1]:
   - **D9 Navamsha Strength:** Allocated exactly 15% weight due to its role as the ultimate sub-divisional truth modifier[cite: 1].
   - **Specific Divisional Chart (Varga):** Allocated exactly 10% weight mapped directly to the query's domain (e.g., D10 for Career, D4 for Property)[cite: 1].
   - **Vimshopaka Overall Shodashavarga Pool:** Allocated exactly 5% weight based on the planet's collective placement dignity across all 16 divisional charts[cite: 1].

## 2. Special Guardrails & Modifiers
- **Karaka Bhava Nashaya Filter:** If the natural Karaka occupies the target query house directly (e.g., Jupiter in the 5th house for progeny, or Venus in the 7th house for marriage), apply an automatic structural modifier/delay flag[cite: 1].
- **Pushkara Navamsha & Vargottama Check:** Planets occupying identical signs in D1 and D9 (Vargottama) or falling within Pushkara Navamsha degrees receive automatic positive modifiers within their respective 30% varga allocation pool[cite: 1].
- **Strict Single-Focus Constraint:** No combined queries are permitted at runtime[cite: 1]. Dual topics are programmatically split into individual independent 100-point calculations[cite: 1].
